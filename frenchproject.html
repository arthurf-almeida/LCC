Les Intouchables

The movie starts with Driss, our main character, in a fancy car with Philippe, Driss is driving recklessly to a place unknown to the audience, they are then stopped by the police, who gets tricked by them into thinking Phihlippe needs to go to the hospital, in this scene we learn that Philippe is paraplegic, when arriving at the hospital, the police go find a wheelchair for Philippe, so they take this chance to leave. The movie then goes back to when Driss and Philippe first met, Driss applied for a job of taking care of Philippe, in which he was hired. In his first day of work, he messes around, doesn't do his job properly, and is later challenged by Philippe to last a month in his job.

After some time, they start to get along and quickly become friends, Driss also makes friends with the other workers, except for Elisa, Philippe's daughter, he also flirts with Magalie, who doesn't give him much attention. Driss discovers that Philippe has a 'lover' who exchanges letters with him, Driss does not understand why they don't go further into their relationship and helps Philippe make some moves. Things start to get better between Phihlippe and his lover, but goes down as their expected date doesn't happen.

Driss quits his job because of family issues and Philippe has to hire someone else, but as time passes, Philippe gets more sad as he misses his friend, until one night, Driss comes back and they both go for a ride, which is where the first scene happens. They get to the airport and fly to another place, where Driss prepares his friend for a date with his lover, which he doesn't know about. There's a funny scene where Driss is shaving Philippe's beard in many different styles, including a square, when they get to the restaurant Driss starts talking and leaves, that's when Philippe's lover comes, the scene then moves to Driss as he is walking and the movie ends.

Review:

Je recommande le film, c'est film est un drame, une comédie, J'ai aimé l'histoire, les personnages et le jeu d'acteur, c'était amusant et intéressant, l'action se passe en France, la fin du film est surprenant, l'intrige est trés simple, la vendette du film est Omar Sy, il joue bien dans le role de Driss.


L'ascension

L'ascension is a movie about a guy named Samy who climbs Mount Everest to show his love for his childhood friend Nadia. The movie starts with Samy and Nadia on a date, Samy wants to date her, but she refuses and challenges him, if he climbed Mount Everest for her, they would date. After some days Samy is prepared to go, he made a partnership with a radiostation and a clothing brand to promote and give updates on his journey. When he gets to Nepal, he finds his hotel and calls the radiostation, where he finds that his journey is becoming famous. The next day, he flights to the world's highest airport at 9,396 feet.

After leaving the airport he meets with his Sherpa Johnny, he helps him carry his bag to the hotel where Samy meets Jeff, he sees what Samy carries and writes him a list of gear to buy. The next day they walk to Namche Bazaar at 11,286 feet, he eats and plays a football match with the local people, then he climbs again, gets separated from his Jeff and has to find his way to the base camp at 17,598 feet, he gets there during night he calls the radiostation, who is surprised and happy as Samy hasn't called in 5 days. The next day Samy tries to climb a wall of ice, but fails miserably as he has no experience, at night he asks his sherpa to teach him how to climb, after many days of training and climbing, he reaches Camp III at 23,497 feet.

At night, as Samy finishes reading his book to Johnny, they start climbing again due to bad weather. When they reach Camp IV at 25,997 feet, they're given oxygen tanks, after a while, he calls Nadia before he goes to the top, when he's close to the top his sherpa tells him to go back as Samy doesn't have enough oxygen, but he won't give up and reaches the top at 29,029 feet, he takes some photos and sends it to the radiostation, where many of his fans are gathered. The movie cuts to Samy going back home being greeted by his family and friends, at last he kisses Nadia, then it shows Johnny recieving a picture of Johnny Hallyday as the final scene.

Review:

Je recommande le film, c'est un film d'aventure, j'aime l'intrigue, les personnages, le décor et le jeu d'acteur, mes personnages préférés étaient Johnny et Samy, c'était amusant, passionant et intéressant, l'action se passe en Népal, la fin du film est passionant, l'intrige est trés simple et les vendettes du filmahmed sont Ahmed Sylla de Samy, Alice Belaidi de Nadia et Kévin Razy de Ben


Lupin

The series start with Assane, our main character, working as a cleaner at the Louvre Museum where he sees a necklace that belonged to Marie-Anroinette, he makes a plan to steal it with some friends, the scene cuts to Assane entering the Louvre as a wealthy man to 'buy' the necklace at the auction. We see his friends infiltrating the Louvre as cleaners and as guards, turning off cameras, knocking down guards, taking their posts, while Assane is bidding for the necklace, we learn that in the past, his father worked as a butler for the family who held the necklace. When he buys the necklace, his friends betray him by stealing it, but soon are caught by the police. The scene cuts to Assane's father being accused of stealing the necklace and being thrown into prison where he commits suicide.

When things start to cool down, a police inspector suspects Assane of being part of the robbery and searches him, but only finds a pen, it then shows his preparation for the robbery, him getting the job as a cleaner, getting a false necklace to trick others and stealing the original one. In the end of the episode he gives his son the same book his father gave him as a kid, Lupin. In episode 2 Assane discovers that the story about his father stealing the necklace is shady, so he decides to find the truth behind it. He goes, disguised as a food deliverer to meet Juliette (daughter of the family who owed the necklace) at a park, he gets some information and flees when he sees an undercover cop, as he escapes other food deliveres show up with the same uniform as him, confusing the police and escaping.

From his father's suicide note he discovers a hidden message that tells him to find Comet, who is in jail, Assane disguises himself as prisioner and gets wounded so he can spend the night at the infirmary to talk to Comet, he learns about a book his father left for him containing information about him. In the book, he finds a message saying "I am innocent, trapped by Anne Pellegrini" (Juliette's mother). Assane thanks Comet for the book and promises him to make his wife smile again, later that night he pretends to commit suicide to escape the prison. The next day he meets Anne, confronts her for framing his father, but she explains that the one pulling the strings was a police officer.

Review:

Je recommande la série, c'est un drame qui se passe en France c'est intrigant, drôle, tendu, excitant, il y a beaucoup de suspense, l'intrigue est complexe et les vedettes de la série sont Omar Sy en Lupin, Etan Simon en Raoul, Antoine Gouy en Benjamin Ferel, Soufiane Guerrab en Youssef Guedira, Vincent Londez en Capitaine Romain Laugier.
